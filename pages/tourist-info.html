💡 Tips for Using Tourist Offices

  • Pick up free maps - They have detailed local maps you can't get online
  • Ask about events - Staff know about local fiestas and events that aren't widely advertised
  • Summer hours are longer - In winter, many offices have reduced hours or are closed
  • They speak English - Most staff speak basic English, some are fluent
  • Restaurant recommendations - Ask where locals eat, not just tourist spots
