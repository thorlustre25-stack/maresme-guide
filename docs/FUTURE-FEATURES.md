## 1. Day Trips & Wine Tourism Section

### Why Add This
- Adds variety beyond beach/party content
- Appeals to families and couples wanting a break from the beach
- Wineries in Alella are only 20 min from the coast
- Many providers already listed on MaresmeEvents serve tourists too

### Potential Content

#### Wine Tourism (Alella DO Region)
| Winery | Location | Activities |
|--------|----------|------------|
| Celler Alta Alella | Alella/Tiana | Vineyard visits, tastings, sea views |
| Celler Bouquet d'Alella | Alella | Guided tours, picnics in vineyards, wellness |
| Celler Quim Batlle | Alella | Tours, tastings, yoga experience |

#### Day Trip Ideas
- Barcelona (already covered in transport)
- Girona (medieval city, ~1hr by train)
- Montserrat (mountain monastery)
- Alella wine region (20 min)
- Tossa de Mar (picturesque Costa Brava town)

### Implementation Notes
- Could be a new page: `pages/day-trips.html`
- Or add as section on Activities page
- Partner with wineries for featured listings?
